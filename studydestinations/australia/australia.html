Necessary Information for Studying in Australia

Before applying to universities in Australia, international students must meet specific requirements, including obtaining a student visa and proving financial and language proficiency.

üìú Student Visa (Subclass 500)

International students must apply for a **Student Visa (Subclass 500)** to study in Australia. The visa allows students to work part-time and stay for the duration of their course.

Visa Details

üìö English Proficiency

Students must demonstrate English proficiency through recognized tests such as:

  • IELTS (Minimum Score: 6.0 - 7.0)
  • TOEFL (Minimum Score: 80 - 100)
  • PTE Academic (Minimum Score: 50 - 65)

üí∞ Financial Requirements

Applicants must show proof of sufficient funds to cover tuition fees, living costs, and return airfare. Estimated costs:

  • Tuition Fees: AUD 20,000 - 45,000 per year
  • Living Costs: AUD 21,000 per year
